Game Overview

Gloomhaven: Jaws of the Lion is a cooperative fantasy campaign game with tactical card-driven combat, character progression, and scenario-based storytelling.

Quick Facts

Age 14+
Players 1–4
Play Time 30–120 min
Category Adventure / Campaign

What Makes It Fun?

Cooperative Play

Players work together to defeat enemies, complete scenarios, and survive difficult choices.

Fantasy Adventure

The game creates a rich world full of monsters, missions, characters, and evolving challenges.

Tactical Decisions

Card choices, movement, timing, and teamwork all matter during combat.

How to Play

1. Choose a Character

Each player controls a unique hero with special cards, abilities, and combat style.

2. Complete Scenarios

Players enter missions, fight enemies, manage resources, and work toward scenario goals.

3. Grow Over Time

Characters improve, the campaign develops, and each session builds on previous choices.

Who Is This Game For?

Strategy Fans

Ideal for players who enjoy planning moves, managing actions, and solving tactical puzzles.

Story Lovers

A strong pick for players who enjoy campaign progression and narrative-driven sessions.

Cooperative Groups

Great for friends who prefer working together instead of competing directly.

Fantasy Gamers

A good fit for players who enjoy fantasy worlds, monsters, heroes, and tactical battles.
