Game Overview

Monopoly is one of the most recognizable family board games, built around buying, trading, collecting rent, and managing money.

Quick Facts

Age 8+
Players 2–6
Play Time 60+ min
Category Family / Classic

What Makes It Fun?

Property Building

Buy properties, build houses and hotels, and increase rent as your empire grows.

Money Management

Players make spending, saving, trading, and risk decisions throughout the game.

Family Competition

A classic choice for groups who enjoy negotiation, luck, and friendly rivalry.

How to Play

1. Move Around the Board

Roll dice, move your token, and land on properties, cards, taxes, or special spaces.

2. Buy and Trade

Purchase properties, negotiate with other players, and build sets of matching colors.

3. Collect Rent

Charge rent when players land on your properties and try to remain financially strong.
