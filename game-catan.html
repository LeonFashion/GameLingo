Game Overview

Catan is a modern board game classic where players collect and trade resources to build roads, settlements, and cities.

Quick Facts

Age 10+
Players 3–4
Play Time 60–90 min
Category Strategy

What Makes It Fun?

Trading

Players negotiate resources, make deals, and decide when to cooperate or compete.

Expansion

Build roads and settlements to claim space and grow your position on the island.

Replayability

The board changes every game, creating fresh strategies and different outcomes.

How to Play

1. Collect Resources

Use dice rolls and board placement to collect brick, wood, wheat, ore, and sheep.

2. Build and Trade

Trade with other players and use resources to build roads, settlements, and cities.

3. Score Victory Points

Earn points through buildings, achievements, and development cards until someone wins.
